    • I am a Postdoctoral Associate at Virginia Tech under the supervision of Dr. Barbara Ryder, working on program analysis and its applications in software security. I received my Ph.D. in 2015 from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Notre Dame, where I was working on source-code analyses, mostly program dependence analysis, for software evolution and reliability, advised by Dr. Raul Santelices and Dr. Douglas Thain. Previously, I did my Masters in Computer Science researching graphics and visaulization and worked for over two years as a software engineer at Baidu Inc. I have also been a National Certified Software System Analyst and a National Certified Senior Software Engineer of China since 2007.

    Research Interests 
    Program analysis, software security, software evolution, software reliability.
