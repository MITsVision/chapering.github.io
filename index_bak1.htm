Chapple H. Cai  

    • I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of Notre Dame, working with Dr. Raul A. Santelices on program analysis topics. Previously, I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science with focus on graphics and visualization from the USM School of Computing and M.E. in Computer Science and Technology with empahsis on computer graphics and vision from the State Key Laboratory of CAD & Graphics at Zhejiang University after got my B.E. in CS from Wuhan University. I had been working for more than two years as a software engineer at Baidu Inc. and have been a Chinese National Certified Software System Analyst since 2007 and Senior Software Engineer since 2003.

    Research Interests 
    Static/dynamic program analysis based software engineering, specifically probabilistic analysis of program change impacts.
